Welcome to Dominion!

A Donald X. Vaccarino game of strategy and ambition

Rules of Dominion

How To Play

Your turn consists of four phases:

Action:

You may play one action card from your hand. Follow the directions on the card, which may instruct you to draw additional cards, gain spending power for your upcoming Buy phase, or give you extra actions to play additional action cards.

Buy:

You may purchase any one card in a pile on the table. To do so, play treasure cards from your hand until the value is at least as high as the purchase price of the card you want to buy.

Cleanup:

The card you purchased, all cards that you played, and any cards remaining in your hand, are all placed in your discard pile. This is done automatically for you.

Draw:

Draw five cards from your deck to replenlish your hand. If there are not enough cards in your deck, shuffle your discard pile, and it becomes the base of your draw pile. This is also done automatically for you at the beginning of your turn.

End Game

The game ends when the last Province is bought. Players count the victory points in their decks, and the player with the most points wins! In case of a tie, whoever went first loses.

Modified Rules

A couple rules have changed so this game can be modified for the web. Normally, there are 10 Kingdom cards to choose from, but I only put eight for design reasons, and you can normally win if three Kingdom Card piles run out, but in this version you can only win by purchasing all provinces. Also, the card 'Cellar' does not permit +1 card per card discarded, but does give the player +1 action and +1 draw.
